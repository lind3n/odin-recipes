Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parm, is a casserole-type dish with chicken in a cheesy marinara sauce. It is one of Mari's favorites. Making your own breaded and air-fried chicken is the most annoying part of the recipe. If you want a crispy texture you can prepare them as individual fried chicken pieces with a smaller amount of sauce and cheese on top on a baking sheet, or you can do the more traditional method and have chicken submerged in sauce and cheese in a baking dish.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 chicken breasts, tenderized with a mallet
  • 1/2 cup of shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 to 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 to 2 cups? of mozarella cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs, italian seasoned is best
  • 2 eggs, whisked
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt, to taste, for chicken
  • ground pepper, to taste, for chicken
  • 1/2 cup of flour

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 425F
  2. Put the flour in a small bowl
  3. Put the eggs in a seperate small bowl
  4. Put panko breadcrumbs in a third small bowl, optionally add a pinch of seasonings like paprika, oregano, chili powder.
  5. Put each chicken breast in the flour bowl and coat it with flour, the dunk in the egg bowl, then dunk in the breadcrumb bowl until covered
  6. Air fry the breaded chicken breasts at 400F for 5-8 minutes, they dont have to be cooked through just to have the outsides crisped and dry
  7. Put a light layer of mozarella cheese and parmesan cheese at the base of a 9x9 baking dish
  8. Add the chicken breasts
  9. Put some parmesan cheese on top of each chicken breast, followed by a generous coating of marinara sauce, followed by covering with mozarella cheese
  10. Bake at 425F for 15-20 minutes, until a good amount of the cheese topping is starting to crisp up and darken
  11. Optionally, after air frying the chicken breasts you can spread them out on a baking tray, cover in parmesan, spoon marinara sauce on top almost to the edges, and then cover with mozarella and bake at 430 for 15-20min. Recipes online say this makes for crispier chicken but I like the heartiness of the casserole-type dish.
