Creamy Shrimp Pasta

Image from saltandlavender

Description

This is Mari's favorite dish and one of the first ones she made for me. It is a creamy sauce with a lemony tang with shrimp and pasta. It's simply amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of shrimp, size to preference
  • 1 lb pasta (I prefer linguini)
  • 2 lemons
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil, approximate for cooking
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil, add a dash of salt, and add pasta
  2. Cook pasta to desired texture, drain, and set aside
  3. Add a small amount of olive oil to a saucepan on high heat and add shrimp when it is hot enough that they sizzle
  4. Add garlic, salt, and pepper to shrimp
  5. Cook shrimp on one side until the spines start to whiten and then flip
  6. Continue cooking shrimp until they have changed color throughout and there is no more translucent parts on the spine
  7. Take the shrimp out of the pan and set aside
  8. In the same pan, add approx 1/2 cup of heavy whipping cream, just enough so that the bottom of the pan is completely covered
  9. Heat it on medium-high heat until it just begins to bubble
  10. Add the shredded parmesan cheese and continue cooking until it melts
  11. Add butter, juice of the 2 lemons, and salt and pepper to taste
  12. Add pasta, mix, and add shrimp. Take off heat
